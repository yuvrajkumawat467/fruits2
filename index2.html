Our Fresh Fruits

Apple

Benefits: Rich in fiber, good for heart, boosts immunity.

Negative Impact: Excess consumption may cause blood sugar spikes.

Banana

Benefits: High in potassium, good for digestion and energy.

Negative Impact: May cause constipation if eaten in excess.

Orange

Benefits: Rich in Vitamin C, boosts immunity.

Negative Impact: High acidity may cause acid reflux.

Grapes

Benefits: Good for heart, rich in antioxidants.

Negative Impact: High sugar content may not be good for diabetics.

Mango

Benefits: Improves digestion, rich in Vitamin A.

Negative Impact: Excess may lead to weight gain.

Watermelon

Benefits: Hydrating, good for skin and heart.

Negative Impact: Overeating can cause bloating.

Our Fresh Vegetables

Carrot

Benefits: Good for eyesight, rich in Vitamin A.

Negative Impact: Excess may turn skin slightly orange (beta-carotene effect).

Broccoli

Benefits: Rich in fiber, supports digestion.

Negative Impact: Can cause bloating in some people.

Tomato

Benefits: High in antioxidants, good for skin.

Negative Impact: Excess acidity may cause stomach irritation.

Cucumber

Benefits: Keeps body hydrated, good for digestion.

Negative Impact: Overeating may lead to dehydration due to diuretic effect.

Spinach

Benefits: High in iron, great for blood health.

Negative Impact: May cause kidney stones if eaten excessively.

Onion

Benefits: Good for immunity, contains antibacterial properties.

Negative Impact: Can cause bad breath or stomach irritation.

Benefits: Rich in fiber, good for heart.

Negative Impact: May cause blood sugar spikes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Fruits are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They help improve digestion, boost immunity, and provide essential nutrients for overall health.

Vegetables provide fiber, vitamins, and minerals that support a healthy digestive system, reduce the risk of chronic diseases, and promote overall well-being.

While fruits are healthy, excessive consumption can lead to high sugar intake, which may cause blood sugar spikes. It is best to eat them in moderation.

Leafy greens, carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers are low in calories and high in fiber, making them excellent choices for weight loss.
